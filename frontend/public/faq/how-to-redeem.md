title: How do I redeem LYT tokens?
category: rewards
---

To redeem your LYT tokens:

1. Connect your Freighter wallet.
2. Go to the **Dashboard** and find a reward you have already claimed.
3. Click **Redeem** on the reward item.
4. Approve the transaction in Freighter.
5. The LYT tokens will be burned and the redemption recorded on-chain.

Note: Redemption is irreversible. Redeemed tokens are burned from your balance.
