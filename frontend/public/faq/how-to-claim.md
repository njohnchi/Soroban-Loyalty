title: How do I claim rewards?
category: rewards
---

To claim rewards from a campaign:

1. Connect your Freighter wallet using the "Connect Wallet" button in the top-right corner.
2. Navigate to the **Dashboard** page.
3. Find an active campaign and click **Claim**.
4. Freighter will prompt you to sign the transaction — approve it.
5. Once confirmed on-chain, your LYT balance will increase.

You can only claim once per campaign per wallet address.
