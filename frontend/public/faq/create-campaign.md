id: create-campaign
title: How do I create a campaign as a merchant?
category: merchants
---

To create a loyalty campaign:

1. Connect your Freighter wallet.
2. Navigate to the **Merchant** page.
3. Fill in the **Reward Amount** (in LYT) and **Duration** (in days).
4. Click **Create Campaign** and approve the transaction in Freighter.
5. Your campaign will appear in the "My Campaigns" list once confirmed on-chain.

Users can then discover and claim rewards from your campaign on the Dashboard.
