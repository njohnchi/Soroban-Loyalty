title: What is Freighter?
category: wallet
---

Freighter is a browser extension wallet for the Stellar network. It lets you securely store your Stellar keys and sign transactions without exposing your private key to websites.

To get started:

1. Install the [Freighter extension](https://www.freighter.app/) for Chrome or Firefox.
2. Create a new wallet or import an existing Stellar account.
3. Switch to the correct network (Testnet or Mainnet) in Freighter settings.
4. Click **Connect Wallet** on SorobanLoyalty to link your account.

Freighter never shares your private key with any website.
